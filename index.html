Troubleshooting

Most issues can be resolved by checking permissions, network connectivity, or the latest transit data refresh.

Live data is missing

Confirm you have a working internet connection. Some GO Transit real-time feeds may be temporarily unavailable or limited for specific trips.

Map location is unavailable

Open iOS Settings, find Transit Scanner, and confirm Location is enabled if you want the app to show your position.

Notifications are silent

Check iOS notification settings and confirm alerts are enabled for Transit Scanner before using active-ride reminders.

FAQ

Answers to common questions about app data, permissions, and the current release.

Is Transit Scanner affiliated with GO Transit or Metrolinx?

No. Transit Scanner is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Metrolinx or GO Transit.

Why does the app ask for location permission?

Location permission is used to show your position on the map and to support optional active-ride get-off reminders when you choose to start a ride.

Does Transit Scanner collect my saved routes or searches?

In version 1.0, saved routes, search history, preferences, and active ride state are stored locally on your device. They are not sent to Transit Scanner servers.

Can I use the app without location access?

Yes. The app can still show routes, stops, schedules, and available real-time information. Features based on your current position may be limited.

Where does transit information come from?

The app uses public GO Transit and Metrolinx data services for schedules, vehicle positions, and service updates where available.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Transit Scanner is designed to keep personal commute data on your device in version 1.0. The app does not sell user data, run advertising tracking, or require a Transit Scanner account.

  • Location is used only when permitted for map position and active ride alerts.
  • Saved routes, preferences, search history, and notification settings are stored locally.
  • Transit schedules and live information are cached locally for performance.
  • The app contacts external transit data services and GitHub-hosted app data files required for functionality.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising tracking are used in version 1.0.
Transit Scanner is an independent app. Public transit data may be delayed, incomplete, or unavailable at times. Always follow official transit signage, announcements, and safety instructions.
